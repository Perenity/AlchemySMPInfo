Alchemy SMP Plugin

Everyone starts off with the ability to only carry 3 potions at a time. Any extra potions beyond that limit are instantly dropped.

To increase your potion capacity, you’ll need to kill other players. Each kill grants you +1 Alchemy, which lets you hold one more potion than before. But be careful dying sets you back, removing -1 Alchemy and lowering your potion limit by one.

If you die while at 0 Alchemy, you go into the negatives and that means an instant death ban.

You can increase your potion limit up to a maximum of 10. So choose your fights wisely… every death or kill has real consequences.

But die? You lose -1 Timepoint.
Drop below 0, and you start slowing down.
Hit -6… and you’re death banned.
